Nimbasia Mobile — Data-Entry Redesign
◆ Mobile Redesign · Direction Pitch

Built for entering data in the field, not reading it.

A clean, professional, app-first mobile experience for Nimbasia. The phone is optimized for the three things your team actually does on the move — logging enquiries, raising orders, and updating production. Reporting and deep tables stay on desktop.

3 priority flows One-thumb reach 44px touch targets Live status updates
Why this works
⚡
Action-first home
The first screen is a launchpad of big, colour-coded entry tiles — not a table. Posting data is one tap away.
📝
Single-column forms
Every field is full-width and thumb-height. Related fields (qty · unit) sit on one row only when they're short.
🎚️
Tap-to-update status
Production jobs change state — Start · Hold · Complete — right from the card. No drilling into menus.
📌
Sticky save bar
The primary action floats at the bottom, always in thumb range, with a confirming toast after every save.

The visual language

Clean and professional — a confident corporate-blue gradient for primary actions, restrained deep tones on the entry tiles, and clear semantic colours so state reads at a glance.
